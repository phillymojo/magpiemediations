How It Works
From Search to Settlement in Four Steps
The entire process — mediator selection, agreement execution, payment, and scheduling — is handled on the platform.
1

Browse & Select

Search our roster of vetted civil litigators by practice area, geography, and availability. View full profiles and select your mediator.

2

Book & Pay

Choose a half-day or full-day session. Set your date, select video or in-person format, and pay securely online — all in one flow.

3

Sign Agreements

All required documents — mediation agreement, confidentiality provisions, and conflicts disclosure — are executed electronically through the platform.

4

Mediate

Connect via secure video conference or meet in person. Your mediator — an active civil litigator — facilitates the session with real-world perspective.

The Opportunity for Active Litigators
You already have the skills. You know how cases settle. Now earn supplemental income doing exactly that — as a neutral.
💰

Earn $1,200–$2,000 Per Day*

Half-day mediators earn $1,200. Full-day mediators earn $2,000. Flat rate, paid within 2 business days of session completion.

📅

You Set Your Availability

Mediate on days that work for your practice schedule. You control your calendar — no minimum session commitments.

🗂️

Zero Administrative Overhead

We handle all scheduling, agreement generation, party communications, and payment collection. You show up and mediate.

🌐

Online or In-Person

Mediate via secure video conference without leaving your office, or meet parties in person in your city.

🏷️

Founding Mediator Status

Early applicants receive a "Founding Mediator" profile designation and enhanced platform visibility during our launch phase.

🤝

No Experience Required

Mediation training is not required — your litigation experience is the credential. We welcome first-time mediators with strong civil trial backgrounds.

Eligibility Requirements

Active bar admission in at least one U.S. jurisdiction
Minimum 5 years of active civil litigation practice
No disciplinary history (current bar standing required)
Execution of Magpie Mediations Mediator Agreement
Completion of conflicts-of-interest screening per session
Annual re-verification of active practice status
